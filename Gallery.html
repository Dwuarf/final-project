Name:
Wizards Pipe

Materials:
Michigan fire cherry, maple, waxed hemp string

Description:
The bowl is made of Michigan fire cherry with a hard maple top cap connected to the stem with waxed hemp string. The stem and mouthpiece are also constructed from hard maple and simply laminated together.

2 / 6

Name:
Gorilla Series

Materials:
Michigan black cherry, Michigan fire cherry and redwood laminate

Description:
This production of pipes is knowns as the Gorilla Series becuase they have a large outer bowl diameter with delivers an exceptionally cool and silky smoke. The bowl is made of Michigan black and fire cherry wood, while the mouthpiece is constructed with a redwood laminate.

The bowl is made the heart woods while the stem and mouthpiece utilize narrow branches from the tree which deliver a lighter colored wood.

4 / 6

Name:
Backpacker

Materials:
Michigan fire cherry, white oak, zebra wood and satine bloodwood

Description:
This pipe sports a bowl made of Michigan fire cherry while the rest is constructed from a variety of materials. This pipe uses zero adhesives and can be taken apart into four seperate pieces which allows this piece to be easily taken apart and packed away. The pipe reflects its owner as he is a traveling man who enjoys admiring cultural artwork from around the world.

5 / 6

Name:
Hunter

Materials:
Walnut, Michigan whitetail deer antler and hard maple

Description:
This pipe brings together very common materials in a unique way. Utilizing the walnut for the bowl, a unique section of an antler from a Michigan whitetail deer as the shank and the hard maple mouthpiece, this pipe embodies the hunting spirit found throughout the State.

6 / 6

Name:
Classic Poker

Materials:
Michigan cherry and hard maple

Description:
This is the artist's personal take on the classic "poker" pipe. Using a precise 5/16th inch diameter and 1 1/2 inch deep bowl, this pipe is the perfect tool to smoke flavorful cavendish tobaccos.
