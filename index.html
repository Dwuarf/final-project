History

Bird Dog Pipe Co.

was originally a side project based out of a garage using simple techniques to create beautiful peices that exert both quality and craftsmanship.

Their elegantly simple designs

work in tandem with high quality locally sourced wood to create a pipe you can be proud of owning.

Objective

At Bird Dog Pipe Co.

we strive to create pieces that are one of a kind and unique to each buyer's specifications. We encourage high cooperation between the artist and the customer in order to deliver the pipe of their dreams.
